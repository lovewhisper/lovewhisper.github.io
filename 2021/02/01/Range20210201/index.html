China’s economic centre of gravity is moving south

The country’s dynamic southern provinces now account for two-thirds of output

  • dynamic adj. 动态的；动力的；动力学的；有活力的 n. 动态；动力
  • account for 对…负有责任；对…做出解释；说明……的原因；导致；（比例）占

“DON’T INVEST beyond Shanhaiguan” has been a popular quip in China for years, referring to a pass in the Great Wall that leads to the north-eastern rust belt. Online pundits have recently updated the maxim to “don’t invest outside the Southern Song”, a dynasty that fell almost 750 years ago whose territory was roughly the same as China’s southern half today. The joke contains a kernel of truth: China’s southern provinces are outperforming northern ones in nearly every economic dimension.

  • quip n. 妙语；嘲弄；讽刺语 vi. 嘲弄；讥讽 vt. 嘲弄
  • refer to 参考；涉及；指的是；适用于
  • rust n. 锈；生锈；[植保] 锈病 vt. 使生锈；腐蚀 vi. 生锈；成铁锈色；变迟钝 n. (Rust)人名；(英)拉斯特；(德、捷、瑞典)鲁斯特；(法)吕斯特
  • belt n. 带；腰带；地带 vt. 用带子系住；用皮带抽打 vi. 猛击 n. (Belt)人名；(英、法、德、西)贝尔特
  • rust belt 铁锈地带，工业衰退地带
  • pundit n. 专家；博学者；梵文学者
  • maxim n. 格言；准则；座右铭 n. (Maxim)人名；(英、罗、俄、哈萨)马克西姆
  • territory n. 领土，领域；范围；地域；版图
  • kernel n. 核心，要点；[计] 内核；仁；麦粒，谷粒；精髓
  • outperform vt. 胜过；做得比……好
  • dimension n. 方面;[数] 维；尺寸；次元；容积 adj. 规格的 vt. 标出尺寸
