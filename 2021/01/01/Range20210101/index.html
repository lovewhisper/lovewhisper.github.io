Boston Dynamics

  • Boston Dynamics 波斯顿动力公司

Boston Dynamics is an American engineering and robotics design company founded in 1992 as a spin-off from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology. Headquartered in Waltham, Massachusetts, Boston Dynamics is owned by the Hyundai Motor Group since 2020.

  • robotic adj. 自动的；机器人的，像机器人的 n. 机器人学
  • founded = launched adj. 有基础的 v. 创立；建立（found的过去分词）
  • spin-off n. 副产品;让产易股,抽资脱离
  • the Massachusetts Institute of Technology 麻省理工学院
  • headquarter vi. 设总部 vt. 将…的总部设在；把…放在总部里
  • its headquarters are/is in Boston
  • with its headquarters in …
  • be owned by 由…拥有
