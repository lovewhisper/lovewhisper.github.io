China Overtakes U.S. as World’s Leading Destination for Foreign Direct Investment

  • overtake ... as 超过...成为

  • Foreign Direct Investment (FDI)

  • destination n. 目的地, 终点 > China overtook the U.S. as the world’s top destination for new foreign direct investment last year, as the Covid-19 pandemic amplifies an eastward shift in the center of gravity of the global economy.

  • top = leading

  • amplify vt. 放大, 扩大；增强 vt. & vi. 详述

  • the center of gravity of the global economy

New investments by overseas businesses into the U.S., which for decades held the No. 1 spot, fell 49% in 2020, according to U.N. figures released Sunday, as the country struggled to curb the spread of the new coronavirus and economc output slumped.

  • hold the No.1 spot 排名第一
  • claim/capture the top spot 夺冠
  • recapture the Blue House 重新夺回青瓦台
  • curb = contain
  • slump vi. 大幅度下降, 暴跌；沉重或突然地落下[倒下] n. 萧条期；〈美〉(个人、球队等的)低潮状态

China, long ranked No. 2, saw direct investments by foreign companies climb 4%, the United Nations Conference on Trade and Development said. Beijing used strict lockdowns to largely contain Covid-19, and China’s gross domestic product grew even as most other major economies contracted last year.

  • foreign companies = overseas businesses
  • largely 很大程度上
  • gross domestic product (GDP)
  • contract vi. 感染 = pick up ；订约；收缩 = shrink vt. 感染；订约；使缩短 n. 合同；婚约
  • He is on a temporary contract.

The 2020 investment numbers underline China’s move toward the center of a global economy long dominated by the U.S.—a shift that accelerated during the pandemic as China has cemented its position as the world’s factory floor and expanded its share of global trade.

  • underline = highlight = underscore 强调
  • a shift that/which accelerated =a shift accelerating
  • a shift accelerated by ...
  • cement n. 水泥；接合剂, 黏合物 vt. 在…上抹水泥；粘牢；巩固, 加强 = consolidate
  • factory floor 世界工厂
  • the lion's share 最大的份额
  • Examples :
    • Beijing is adopting / considering / weighing measures to scale back its investment in the US , a shift accelerated by a wideing rift / division / split with Washington since Trump came into office / power.
      • scale back 缩减
      • since Trump came into office / power = since Trump took office = since Trump assumed office = since Trump was in office = since Trump took his presidency
