Check Out US President’s Rarely Seen Official State Car, Bulletproof Chevrolet Suburban SUV

  • Chevrolet 雪佛兰 Cruz 克鲁兹
  • General Motors
  • General Electric
  • bulletproof adj. 防弹的
  • suburban adj. 土气的；郊区的，城郊的；见闻不广的 n. 郊区居民
  • Sport Utility Vehicle (SUV) 越野车
  • pickup truck 皮卡

It’s no news that the US presidential limousine, popularly known as “the Beast” and “Cadillac One”, is the official state car of the president of the United States.

  • It’s no news that ... = ...已经不是新闻
  • limousine n. 豪华轿车
  • popularly known as 俗称

The heavily armoured Cadillac-branded vehicle is one of the safest presidential car on the planet.

  • armoured adj. 装甲的； 配备有装甲车辆的
  • heavily armoured 重装甲的
  • heavily armed 全副武装的

The current model, which debuted on September 24, 2018, has been spotted in the midst of presidential motorcades that range between 24–45 vehicles.

  • debute n. 〈法〉演员首次演出
  • in the midst of 在...之中
  • motorcade n. 一长列汽车,(汽)车队
  • range between 介于

But there is another rarely seen US President’s official state car : a bulletproof Chevrolet Suburban SUV, which the President normally use when his trip within or outside the country requires going off-road.

  • go off-road 越野

The SUV was used to ferry Donald Trump during the president’s surprise trip to Afghanistan to visit U.S. troops in 2019.

  • ferry n. 渡口；渡船；摆渡 vt. （乘渡船）渡过；用渡船运送；空运 vi. 摆渡；来往行驶
  • surprise trip 突访
  • Afghanistan n. 阿富汗
  • troop n. 军队, 部队；一群, 一队 vi. 成群结队地走
  • vet vt. 诊疗；审查 =visit vi. 作兽医；诊疗 n. 兽医
  • the Patriot Party 爱国者党
