Elon Musk is now the richest person in the world, passing Jeff Bezos

  • passing = topping = surpassing
  • Elon Musk 埃隆·马斯克，企业家、慈善家，出生于南非，毕业于宾夕法尼亚大学，以SpaceX的创办者及特斯拉汽车和PayPal的联合创办人而闻名，是2019年霍金科学传播奖获得者，现任SpaceX CEO兼CTO，被誉为“硅谷钢铁侠”。
  • Jeff Bezos 杰夫·贝佐斯，企业家，出生于美国新墨西哥州阿尔布奎克，毕业于普林斯顿大学，互联网巨头亚马逊公司创始人、董事长兼CEO，华盛顿邮报大股东之一。
  • leave Jack Ma in the dust

Elon Musk just became the richest person in the world, with a net worth of more than $185 billion.

  • net n. 网；净利；网络；实价 vi. 编网 vt. 净赚；得到；用网捕 adj. 纯粹的；净余的
  • ultra-high-net-worth individuals 超高净值个体
  • ultra-thin/slim 超薄

Thursday's increase in Tesla's share price pushed Musk past Jeff Bezos, who had been the richest person since 2017 and is currently worth about $184 billion.

  • the auto industry disruptor
  • disruptor 破坏者

Musk's wealth surge over the past year marks the fastest rise to the top of the rich list in history — and is a dramatic financial turnaround for the famed entrepreneur who just 18 months ago was in the headlines for Tesla's rapid cash burn and his personal leverage against the company's stock.

  • surge n. (感情等)洋溢, 奔放；浪涛般汹涌奔腾 vi. (人群等)蜂拥而出；(感情等)洋溢, 奔放；(波涛等)汹涌, 奔腾
  • rise = ascend
  • be in the headlines 新闻头条
  • hit / make / grab the headlines
  • grab vt. & vi. 抢先, 抢占 vt. 攫取, 抓住；对…有好感, 对…产生影响 n. 试图抓取, 有意抢夺
  • cash cow 摇钱树
  • leverage n. 〈物〉杠杆作用, 杠杆的力量；力量, 影响 vt. 促使…改变
  • tech phenomenon
  • market cap = market value = market capitalization 市值
