Trump Farewell Message

  • farewell 告别

We did what we came here to do and so much more. This week we inaugurate a new administration and pray for its success in keeping America safe and prosperous. We extend our best wishes and we also want them to have luck.

  • inaugurate 开始，开创，为...举行开幕式、就职典礼

  • extend our best wishes 致以我们最良好的祝愿 > All Americans were horrified by the assault on our Capitol. Political violence is an attack on everything we cherish as Americans. It can never be tolerated. Now more than ever we must unify around our shared values and rise above the partisan rancor.

  • be horrified by 对...感到震惊

  • assault n. v. 攻击

  • Capitol n.美国国会大厦

  • now more than ever 现在比以往任何时候都多

  • unify v. 团结，统一

  • shared values 共享价值

  • rise above 超越

  • the partisan rancor 党派斗争

  • Examples:

    同样重要的是，我们必须竭尽全力维护我们作为文明社会成员所珍视的一切。

    Equally important is that we must do all in our power to preserve everything we cherish as members of a civilized society.
