It’s been one year since a helicopter crash claimed Bryant, his daughter Gianna and seven others, sending shock and sorrow through the basketball world. To NBA veterans, Bryant was an admired, respected and feared rival. To younger players, he was an icon, a role model and, to many, a valued mentor.

  • a helicopter crash 直升机坠毁
  • helicopter n. 直升机
  • crash n. 碰撞；崩溃；坠落 vt. 破碎；撞碎；坠落 vi. 碰撞；坠毁；闯；突然倒台 adj. 速成的
  • claim vt. 声称, 断言；(灾难等)使失踪或死亡；需要, 值得 vt. & vi. 对…提出要求, 索取 n. 主张, 断言；要求
  • claim the lives of 夺走...的生命
  • veteran n. 经验丰富的人; 老兵；退伍军人
  • icon n. 图标；肖像，画像；圣像；偶像
  • mentor n. (无经验之人的)有经验可信赖的顾问
  • Examples:
    • An array of reports on the murder case have triggered multiple copycat crimes in Calidornia , sending shock waves through the Golden State.
      • an array of = an avalanche of 大量
      • avalanche n. 雪崩 vi. 雪崩;雪崩般塌落 vt. (因大量涌至而)把...淹没,使无法应
      • trigger vt. 触发；引发，引起 vi. 松开扳柄 n. 触发器；扳机；制滑机
      • copycat n. 盲目模仿者
      • send shock waves through 使...受到冲击
    • His Mamba Mentality will live on forever in many of us.
    • 我们分手已经一年了。
    • It's been a year since we broke up.
      • It's been a year since Kobe Bryant departed his life.
