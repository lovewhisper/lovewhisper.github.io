Meet Kyuta: the 10-year-old, 85-kilo sumo in training

  • sumo 相扑
  • sumo wrestling 相扑
  • sumo wrestler n. 相朴运动员

TOKYO, Jan 19 (Reuters) - Among the young sumo wrestlers lifting weights in the ring, 10-year-old Kyuta Kumagai stands out.

  • wrestler n. 摔跤选手，搏斗者
  • wrestle n. 摔跤；斗争；搏斗 v. 斗争；摔跤；斟酌

At 85 kilograms (187 lb), Kyuta is twice the size of the other children his age and is so dominant that he wrestles, and beats, boys five or six years older.

Last year, he was crowned the under-10 world champion, beating out competition from as far afield as the United Kingdom and Ukraine.

  • crown n. 王冠；王权；花冠；顶点 vt. 加冕；居…之顶；表彰；使圆满完成
  • champion n. 冠军；捍卫者, 拥护者
  • defending champion 卫冕冠军
  • world championship 世界竞标赛
  • beat out 击败
  • competition n. 比赛；竞争；竞争对手=competitor = rival
  • afield adv. 在战场上；去野外；在远处；远离著
  • as far afield as 远至
  • Ukraine 乌克兰

His training regime, devised by his father Taisuke, is relentless.

  • regime n. 政权，政体；社会制度；管理体制
  • devise vt. 想出; 计划; 设计; 发明
  • relentless adj. 残酷的, 不留情的, 无怜悯心的

He trains six days a week, either at his local sumo club or lifting weights. He also swims and practices track and field to build up the flexibility and explosive quickness needed for sumo wrestling.

  • track and field 田径

  • build up 增强

  • flexibility n. 柔韧性；机动性，灵活性

  • inflexibility n. 不屈性,顽固,不变性

  • explosive adj. 爆炸的, 爆发的；极易引起争论的 n. 爆炸物, 炸药

‘Deepfake’ Queen delivers alternative Christmas speech, in warning about misinformation

  • deepfake = deep learning + fake
  • fake vt. 伪造, 篡改, 对…做手脚；仿造 vt. & vi. 假装 n. 骗子；赝品 adj. 假的, 冒充的
  • alternative adj. 两者择一的, 供替代的；不寻常的, 非传统性的；非正统的 n. 取舍, 抉择, 可供选择的事物；选择的余地, 可供选择的机会
  • alternative music 非主流音乐
  • alternative right 另类右派（美国），另类保守派
  • alternative fuel 替代性燃料

London(CNN) A fake Queen Elizabeth danced across TV screens on Christmas as part of a “deepfake” speech aired by a British broadcaster.

  • as part of 作为…的一部分
  • Google acquired DeepLearning as part of its ambition to expand into AI.
  • air vt. & vi.播送, 广播

The real British monarch traditionally delivers a Christmas Day speech aired around the world.

  • monarch n. 君主, 帝王
  • around the world = the world over = worldwide

But her speech on Friday at 3 p.m. was followed by a digitally-created fake of the Queen, aired on Channel 4 and voiced by an actor, warning viewers to question “whether what we see and hear is always what it seems.”

  • be followed by 被…跟随

The broadcaster said the video was supposed to offer “a stark warning about the advanced technology that is enabling the proliferation of misinformation and fake news in a digital age.”

  • stark adj. 僵硬的, 光秃秃的, 荒凉的；完全的, 全然的
  • in stark contrast = in sharp contrast 形成鲜明对比的是
  • proliferation n. 增殖，扩散；分芽繁殖

Deepfake technology can be used to create convincing yet entirely fictional video content, and is often used to spread misinformation.

  • fictional adj. 虚构的;小说的
  • moderate the content 审核内容
  • content moderator

Channel 4 annually accompanies the Queen’s traditional speech with an “alternative Christmas message.” This message has been aired since 1993.

  • accompany vt. 陪伴, 陪同；伴随…同时发生；伴奏
  • accompany A with B
  • Buckingham Palace declined to comment on the alternative Christmas message.
  • decline n. 下降, 减少, 衰退 vt. & vi. 辞谢; 谢绝(邀请等) vi. (太阳)落下
  • Downing Street = No. 10 = London 指代英国政府
