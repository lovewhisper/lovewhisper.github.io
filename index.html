Meet Kyuta: the 10-year-old, 85-kilo sumo in training

  • sumo 相扑
  • sumo wrestling 相扑
  • sumo wrestler n. 相朴运动员

TOKYO, Jan 19 (Reuters) - Among the young sumo wrestlers lifting weights in the ring, 10-year-old Kyuta Kumagai stands out.

  • wrestler n. 摔跤选手，搏斗者
  • wrestle n. 摔跤；斗争；搏斗 v. 斗争；摔跤；斟酌

At 85 kilograms (187 lb), Kyuta is twice the size of the other children his age and is so dominant that he wrestles, and beats, boys five or six years older.

Last year, he was crowned the under-10 world champion, beating out competition from as far afield as the United Kingdom and Ukraine.

  • crown n. 王冠；王权；花冠；顶点 vt. 加冕；居…之顶；表彰；使圆满完成
  • champion n. 冠军；捍卫者, 拥护者
  • defending champion 卫冕冠军
  • world championship 世界竞标赛
  • beat out 击败
  • competition n. 比赛；竞争；竞争对手=competitor = rival
  • afield adv. 在战场上；去野外；在远处；远离著
  • as far afield as 远至
  • Ukraine 乌克兰

His training regime, devised by his father Taisuke, is relentless.

  • regime n. 政权，政体；社会制度；管理体制
  • devise vt. 想出; 计划; 设计; 发明
  • relentless adj. 残酷的, 不留情的, 无怜悯心的

He trains six days a week, either at his local sumo club or lifting weights. He also swims and practices track and field to build up the flexibility and explosive quickness needed for sumo wrestling.

  • track and field 田径

  • build up 增强

  • flexibility n. 柔韧性；机动性，灵活性

  • inflexibility n. 不屈性,顽固,不变性

  • explosive adj. 爆炸的, 爆发的；极易引起争论的 n. 爆炸物, 炸药

  • Examples：

    Corporate China is racing to build up a presence in Africa.

    • be racing to 争夺
    • race / rush / scramble

Trump Farewell Message

  • farewell 告别

We did what we came here to do and so much more. This week we inaugurate a new administration and pray for its success in keeping America safe and prosperous. We extend our best wishes and we also want them to have luck.

  • inaugurate 开始，开创，为…举行开幕式、就职典礼
  • extend our best wishes 致以我们最良好的祝愿

All Americans were horrified by the assault on our Capitol. Political violence is an attack on everything we cherish as Americans. It can never be tolerated. Now more than ever we must unify around our shared values and rise above the partisan rancor.

  • be horrified by 对…感到震惊

  • assault n. v. 攻击

  • Capitol n.美国国会大厦

  • now more than ever 现在比以往任何时候都多

  • unify v. 团结，统一

  • shared values 共享价值

  • rise above 超越

  • the partisan rancor 党派斗争

  • Examples:

    同样重要的是，我们必须竭尽全力维护我们作为文明社会成员所珍视的一切。

    Equally important is that we must do all in our power to preserve everything we cherish as members of a civilized society.

    It is equally important that , we must do all in our power to preserve everything we cherish as members of a civilized society.

    • do all in our power 竭尽全力，尽我们所能
    • Equally important is that , … = It is equally important that , …

The ‘Trump Baby’ blimp that dogged the President for years will live on in a museum (CNN)

  • blimp 软式小型飞船，肥胖的人
  • dog v.困扰

A giant blimp depicting Donald Trump as a diaper-clad baby, which followed the outgoing US President on his visits to London and symbolized international opposition to his administration, has secured its place in history after being acquired by a British museum.

  • depict 描绘
  • diaper 尿布
  • outgoing = departing 即将离任的 lame duck
  • symbolize 象征着
  • has secured its place 保住一席之地
  • National Guard 国民警卫队
  • vex 使苦恼

The 20-foot-tall “Trump Baby” blimp was created ahead of the President’s first visit to the UK, when hundreds of thousands of Britons poured onto the country’s streets to protest his presence in the country.

  • sniper 狙击者
  • Deep State 隐形政府
  • ahead of = prior to = before

It will now be displayed in the Museum of London alongside other remnants of public protests in the British capital, the institution announced on the eve of Trump’s departure from the Oval Office.

  • remnant n.残余 adj.残余的
  • on the eve of 在…前夕
  • departure from his life
